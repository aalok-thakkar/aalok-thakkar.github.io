Course Project Guidelines:


Module 3 covers solvers for Boolean satisfiability problem (SAT) and satisfiability modulo theories (SMT). The objective of the project is to apply these solvers to a domain of your choice. The project proposal must be submited by September 28 and approved by the instructor. Each project check-ins require you to summarize the project in a brief email. The project submission should include a short write-up summarizing your approach and outcomes and an infographic that summarizes your project. The project presentation should be a 15 minute talk on your work.
