I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Ashoka University, and an Alt Carbon Darjeeling Revival Fellow. My research lies at the intersection of logic, programming languages, and artificial intelligence. I am particularly interested in trustworthy AI, computational sustainability, and computer science pedagogy.

In 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer and Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania, where I was advised by Rajeev Alur and Mayur Naik. During this time, I developed the example-guided framework for synthesis of relational queries.
