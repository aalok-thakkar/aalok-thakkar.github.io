I am an assistant professor in the department of Computer Science at Ashoka University, and an Alt Carbon Darjeeling Revival Fellow. I specialize in the areas of programming languages, formal logic, and artificial intelligence. My current work focusses on trustworthy AI, computational sustainability, and CS pedagogy.

In 2023, I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Rajeev Alur and Mayur Naik. During this time, I developed the example-guided framework for synthesis of relational queries.

In the past, I have worked with Adobe Systems, Aptos Labs, Amazon AWS, and Bell Labs.
